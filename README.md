# 🛰️ OrbitalOps

# Operational Intelligence for the Next Era of Space Infrastructure

OrbitalOps is a next-generation aerospace operations platform focused on constellation awareness, relay network coordination, telemetry intelligence, anomaly detection, mission continuity, and AI-native operational infrastructure.

## 🌍 Mission

Humanity is entering an era where operational infrastructure will increasingly extend beyond the boundaries of Earth. OrbitalOps explores how future operators may understand, coordinate, and protect complex aerospace ecosystems consisting of satellites, relay networks, ground stations, autonomous systems, and AI-assisted operational environments.

Rather than treating mission operations as a collection of isolated dashboards, OrbitalOps approaches aerospace operations as a unified operational intelligence problem.

---

## 🚀 Why OrbitalOps Exists

Modern orbital infrastructure is growing rapidly.

The next generation of operational environments will likely involve:

- Large-scale satellite constellations
- Autonomous relay networks
- Persistent telemetry streams
- AI-assisted diagnostics
- Lunar communications infrastructure
- Deep-space logistics systems
- Planetary-scale operational awareness

Traditional mission-control systems were designed for a different scale.

OrbitalOps explores what aerospace operations could look like when thousands of assets, millions of telemetry events, and intelligent operational systems operate together within a unified environment.

## ☀️ Solar Flare Event Walkthrough

The centerpiece of the demonstration is a simulated solar flare event.

### Phase 1 — Nominal Operations

Operators observe:

- Healthy assets
- Stable telemetry
- Normal routing
- Healthy communications

### Phase 2 — Early Indicators

The platform begins detecting:

- Signal degradation
- Thermal drift
- Latency increases
- Packet loss

### Phase 3 — Event Escalation

The solar event impacts multiple assets simultaneously.

Relay utilization increases.

Affected satellites enter degraded operational states.

Network health begins to decline.

### Phase 4 — Operational Response

OrbitalOps demonstrates:

- Anomaly correlation
- Route analysis
- Recovery recommendations
- Continuity evaluation

### Phase 5 — Recovery

Infrastructure stabilizes.

Network health improves.

Mission continuity is preserved.
